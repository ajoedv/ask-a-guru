4. Cookies & Analytics

We use essential cookies to run the site and (optionally) analytics cookies to understand usage. Where required, we will ask for your consent via a cookie banner. You can adjust preferences at any time.

5. Offline Payments & Invoicing

We do not process card payments on this website. If you choose to pay via offline methods (e.g., bank transfer/Swish/cash/invoice), we may collect basic billing details (name, company, VAT number if provided), invoice totals, payment status, and method for accounting and legal purposes. No card details are handled on this site.

6. Data Sharing

We may share minimal data with trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, email delivery, analytics) under data processing agreements. We do not sell your personal data.

7. International Transfers

If data is transferred outside the EEA/UK, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs) as required by law.

8. Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described, or to comply with legal obligations. We then delete or anonymize it.

9. Your Rights (EU/EEA/UK)

To exercise your rights, contact us at info@ask-a-guru.com (or joedev@ask-a-guru.com). We may need to verify your identity.

10. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

11. Children

12. Changes to This Policy

13. Contact
